## Method notes for the operator

- Cross-checked every candidate against the existing montreal + quebec_city arrays in `scripts/_ca-clinics-by-metro.json` by both name AND website domain. No duplicates included.
- Emails are NEVER fabricated. Where I could not see an email literally quoted in a search-result snippet, the email field is `null`. Several listings show a real email that appeared verbatim in a snippet (e.g., GTR Santé, Clinique Nord, MCIM was excluded because closed, Nela Santé phone-only, etc.).
- I deliberately rejected several plausible-sounding leads that turned out to be Ontario/BC/Alberta only (Centre for Advanced Medicine = Markham; Essence of Health Clinic = Hamilton; Health Globe Wellness = Mississauga; MMC Wellness = BC; LUXX Aesthetics = Barrie/Bobcaygeon ON; Signature Beauty Lounge = Toronto/Richmond Hill; Clinic 360 = Toronto; Hydra Med Spa = Abbotsford BC).
- I also rejected leads that did not visibly confirm an IV menu (Mediluxe, Station L, Aura Dermatologie, Sensolia, Confort Physio, Clinique PV, Mouvement Humain, Clinique Altermed, Centre de Solution Santé, Luxe Dermato Esthétique, Derma MD Brossard, Dermapure Westmount, Dermapure Pointe-Claire, Dermapure Sainte-Foy, Cliniques VIVA, Clinique VIP Québec, Cliniques médicales Lacroix, Polyclinique Ste-Foy, Epiderma Sainte-Foy, Medicart Québec, Mobile IV Canada national page, Drip Hydration Quebec City — DH only lists Montreal/Toronto/Vancouver in Canada).
- I rejected Montreal Center for Integrative Medicine (mcim.ca) — search confirmed it closed in June 2017.
- "Drug-infusion-only" clinics serving prescribed biologics (Bayshore, Innomar, Summit Health iron/B12-only, Groupe effiScience, Alpha Soins Infirmiers, Servir Plus, Ligne de Vie, Clinique Soins Santé GC) were excluded because they do prescription drug infusion under MD referral, not wellness IV — out of scope per the brief.
- All websites listed below resolved in the search index (i.e., search engines have crawled live pages, not "for sale" parking).
