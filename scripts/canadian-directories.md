## Top 10 Canadian Directories

### 1. YellowPages.ca
- Homepage: https://www.yellowpages.ca
- Submission URL: https://solutions.yp.ca/free-online-listing
- Business management portal: https://ypforbusiness.yellowpages.ca/
- Free / Paid: **Free** (paid upgrades available)
- DA: **High** (~85-90)
- Account required: **Yes** — email + business profile login
- Notes: The single most important Canadian citation. Free listing form is short, but human-reviewed; expect 1–7 business days for the listing to go live. Often syndicates to Canada411 automatically. Pick "Health & Wellness" or "Information Services" as the category; use "Online Directory" in the description.

### 2. Canada411
- Homepage: https://www.canada411.ca
- Submission URL: https://www.canada411.ca/ (use the "Free Business Profile" button top-right) — also fed automatically via YellowPages.ca submissions
- Free / Paid: **Free**
- DA: **High** (~80)
- Account required: **Yes** — requires a working phone number for verification
- Notes: Owned by the same company as YellowPages.ca (Yellow Pages Group). If you already submit to YP.ca, the listing typically appears here within a few days. If submitting directly, you'll need a Canadian phone number, business address, province, postal code and an industry pick.

### 3. BBB Canada (Better Business Bureau)
- Homepage: https://www.bbb.org/ca
- Free listing submission URL: https://www.bbb.org/get-listed
- Paid accreditation URL (optional): https://www.bbb.org/apply
- Free / Paid: **Free listing** (accreditation is a paid annual program — skip for now)
- DA: **Very High** (~93)
- Account required: **Yes** — email + business info; phone verification possible
- Notes: BBB requires the business to be at least 6 months old to be listed. Since TheDripMap was founded in 2026, **wait until ~Nov 2026** before submitting, or submit immediately and expect possible rejection on the age rule. Pick category "Online Directory" or "Health Information & Referral Services." Listing only — do NOT pay for accreditation yet.

### 4. Yelp Canada
- Homepage: https://www.yelp.ca
- Submission URL: https://biz.yelp.com/claim (works for both .com and .ca)
- Business login: https://biz.yelp.ca/login
- Free / Paid: **Free** (ads are paid, ignore them)
- DA: **Very High** (~94)
- Account required: **Yes** — email signup, then phone call/text verification to a real number
- Notes: Yelp may push back on directory-style businesses (their guidelines prefer physical-location businesses). Frame TheDripMap as an "Online Health Resource" or "Local Service" with a Canadian HQ address. Approval can take a few hours to a few days. Free Business Page includes reviews, hours, photos, and a CTA URL — claim it before competitors do.

### 5. Hotfrog Canada
- Homepage: https://www.hotfrog.ca
- Submission URL: https://www.hotfrog.ca/ — scroll to the green "Add your business" button at the bottom of the homepage
- Free / Paid: **Free** (Basic Profile); Advantage Profile is $20/mo, skip
- DA: **High** (~70)
- Account required: **Yes** — email used for verification; confirmation email arrives within 30 min
- Notes: Asks for Business Name, Town, Province, Postal Code, Business Email, and Keywords. Excellent for general SEO because Hotfrog syndicates listings to other directories. Allows online-only / non-physical businesses. Approval is automated after email verification.

### 6. n49.ca
- Homepage: https://www.n49.com (Canada listings primarily at n49.ca)
- Submission URL: https://www.n49.com/ — "Add A Business" button, top right of the homepage
- Free / Paid: **Free** plan available (paid upgrades for reviews/management)
- DA: **High** (~60)
- Account required: **Yes** — free account creation
- Notes: Canadian-focused review and listing directory. Requires Street Address, Postal Code, Province, City, and Phone. Optional fields: Suite #, Neighbourhood, Email, Toll-Free Number, Fax, Website. Pick "Health & Wellness" main category and "Online Directory" or "Medical Service" subcategory. Will allow online-only businesses if a Canadian address is provided.

### 7. Cylex Canada
- Homepage: https://www.cylex-canada.ca
- Submission URL: https://www.cylex-canada.ca/companyregistration.html (registration via "Register" link in header)
- Free / Paid: **Free** core listing; bulk upload is ~$2/listing/year
- DA: **Medium-High** (~55)
- Account required: **Yes** — email signup
- Notes: Free listings include menus/brochures/price-list uploads, video, and a service/product catalog — useful for showcasing IV therapy categories. Approval typically within 1–3 days. Lenient toward online-only / directory businesses.

### 8. Brownbook.net (Canada)
- Homepage: https://www.brownbook.net/country-selector/ca/
- Submission URL: https://www.brownbook.net/add-business
- Free / Paid: **Free**
- DA: **High** (~70)
- Account required: **No account strictly required** to add — but creating one lets you edit/claim later. Email recommended.
- Notes: Global open-edit directory (wiki-style). Listings go live almost instantly with no human review, which makes it fast but means any user can edit your listing — claim the entry after creation. Excellent DA for the effort. Allows online businesses.

### 9. 411.ca (separate from Canada411)
- Homepage: https://411.ca
- Submission URL: https://411.ca/add-your-business
- Free / Paid: **Free** (premium visibility upgrades available)
- DA: **Medium-High** (~55-60)
- Account required: **Yes** — phone number used to register the listing
- Notes: Distinct site from canada411.ca. Indexes over 1.1M Canadian businesses and feeds local Google search visibility. Accepts online services and directories. Submission is fast (~5 min) and listings typically go live within 24–72 hours.

### 10. Lacartes (Canada)
- Homepage: https://www.lacartes.com
- Submission / signup URL: https://www.lacartes.com/join
- Free / Paid: **Free**
- DA: **Medium** (~45-50)
- Account required: **Yes** — free account creation
- Notes: International business directory with strong Canadian coverage. Allows photos, price lists, menus, videos, customer reviews, and wall posts — friendly to non-physical businesses. Free signup, instant listing, no manual approval queue. Use the "Health & Beauty" or "Health Services" category.

## Submission order recommendation

Start with the **Big 4** — YellowPages.ca, Yelp Canada, Hotfrog Canada, and n49.ca — these give you the highest combined DA boost and feed downstream directories (especially YP.ca → Canada411 syndication). Knock out Brownbook and Lacartes second since they're near-instant approvals with no friction and pad out your citation count quickly. Save BBB Canada for **November 2026 or later** (6-month minimum business age), and do Cylex, 411.ca, and Canada411 (if not auto-populated) in any order over the following week — they take 5–15 minutes each.
